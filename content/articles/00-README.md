## How to credit CC BY sources on the site

Each article ends with a "Sources" section. To stay compliant on a non-commercial site, place a "Sources & Credits" page (see `sources-and-credits.md`) reachable from the footer of every page. Every CC BY credit line must include:

1. Title of the work
2. Author(s)
3. Source / publisher
4. License (CC BY 4.0)
5. URL

## Re-verify before publishing

A few sources are flagged "verify license" in this pack. Before publishing:

- Click each link to confirm it is live.
- For "verify license" sources, confirm the exact license on the publisher's own page.
- Re-verify carbon-tax rates in `main-content-pack.md` against the OECD database yearly.

See `sources-and-credits.md` for the full compliance guide.
